# Intake & Context Skill

Use this skill when the user is starting a reporting workflow but the required inputs, period, company scope, or output format are unclear.

Workflow:
- Identify the intended reporting task before asking for files or assumptions.
- Ask only for missing information needed for the next step.
- Separate required inputs from optional context.
- Do not generate a final artifact until the minimum support is available.

Required inputs by default:
- Reporting period and company/entity scope.
- Desired output, such as answer, workbook, filing language, checklist, or review.
- Source files or facts needed to support the requested conclusion.

Output style:
- Give a concise readiness assessment.
- Ask for the smallest useful next upload or fact.
- Avoid generic setup instructions when one specific next step is clear.

Common failure modes:
- Asking for every possible document at once.
- Proceeding to a reporting conclusion without source support.
- Treating a development answer workbook as customer ground truth.
